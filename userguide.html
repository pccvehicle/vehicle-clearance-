Why do I need location verification for the APOs‚Äô Dashboard? +

This APOs‚Äô feature ensures only authorized personnel at approved locations can access sensitive vehicle clearance data, preventing remote unauthorized access.

üîß Troubleshooting Guide

Quick solutions to common issues.

Access & Login Issues

Problem: Cannot access APOs‚Äô Dashboard.

Solutions: Verify your User ID is correct, you are at an authorized location with location services enabled, and you have an internet connection. Contact an admin to check your account status.

Search & Data Issues

Problem: Vehicle not found in search.

Solutions: Double-check the vehicle number, ensure the clearance is for today's date, and confirm the request was approved.

Mobile Device Issues

Problem: Location services not working.

Solutions: Enable location in your device and browser settings. For a better GPS signal, try moving outdoors. Restarting your device may also help.

System Performance Issues

Problem: Slow loading or real-time updates not working.

Solutions: Check your internet connection. Try refreshing the page or clearing your browser cache. If the issue persists, contact an administrator.
