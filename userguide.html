üìù Submitting a Clearance Request

This section details the complete step-by-step process for requesting vehicle clearance.

Key Submission Rules

‚ö†Ô∏è Important Cut-Off Time: To be eligible for same-day clearance, all requests must be submitted before 14:00 (2:00 PM) local time. Requests submitted after this time must be for the next day or later.

Step-by-Step Process

1

Access the Form & Fill Details

Click "Request Clearance" on the main page. Enter your name, a valid email address for notifications, contact number, vehicle number, and vehicle type.

2

Select Visiting Hours & Dates

Choose your intended visiting time slot from the dropdown menu. Then, select the entry date, keeping the 2 PM cut-off time in mind.

3

Submit and Check Your Email

Review all information for accuracy and click "Submit". You will receive an email confirming that your request has been received and is pending review.

After Submission

Once submitted, your request is sent to administrators for review. You can track the status on the main page, and you will receive a final email notification once it is either approved or rejected.

‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

What is the daily cut-off time for new requests? +

For a request to be considered for same-day access, it must be submitted before 14:00 (2:00 PM). Any requests made after this time must be for the following day or later.

How will I be notified about my request's status? +

You will receive automated emails. The first email confirms your submission is pending. A second email will inform you if your request has been approved or rejected. You can also use the "Track Request" feature on the main page.

Can administrators generate reports? +

Yes. Administrators can generate a 24-hour PDF report from their dashboard. This report lists all currently active and pending clearances and is sorted by time slots, making it ideal for daily briefings and shift handovers.

Why do I need to select a time slot? +

Selecting a visiting time slot helps administrators manage parking availability and operational capacity, ensuring a smoother and more organized entry process for everyone.
